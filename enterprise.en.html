We’re focused on keeping your data safe and secure

  • Your data is hosted by OVH, Europe’s largest hosting provider that maintains the highest level of security standard and certification.

  • Audit and follow-up reports can be read on demand.

  • We use HTTPS protocol with TLSv1.2 or higher in transit and AES 256 at rest.

  • On demand, we can connect the logs to your existing system, so that you can use your own alerts and monitoring.

  • You can enable sign in by users from specific IPs only to prevent outside access to your data while enabling flexible collaboration inside your organization.

  • You can whitelist email addresses related to specific domains to prevent your team members form.

  • You can disable users from sharing boards via public links to prevent leaks of sensitive information.

  • Backups are processed daily and stocked for one year in a separate location.
